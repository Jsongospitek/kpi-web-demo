How it works

  • Upload CSV → KPIs are computed per day, then WEEK_AVG is added.
  • Negative values in OR-cycle components are ignored per cell (treated as missing).
  • Minor discrepancies (around ±1%) may occur due to differences in rounding logic.
  • Outliers are automatically filtered out during the calculation process, which may lead to differences from the analytics results.
